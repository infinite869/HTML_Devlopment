About: The Deloitte Football Money League is a ranking of football clubs by revenue generated from football operations. It is the most contemporary and reliable analysis of the clubs’ relative financial performance. It is produced annually by the accountancy firm Deloitte and released in early February of each year, describing the season most recently finished.
